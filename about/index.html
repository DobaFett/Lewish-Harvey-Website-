About Lewis-Harvey

Electrical installation contractors, based at Kingston Park in Newcastle and trading since 2002. Specialists in electrical installations with a modern, reliable service — for homes, businesses and public organisations across the North East.

Who we are

A time-served team that does what it says

Lewis-Harvey has been installing, testing and maintaining electrical systems since 2002. Our technicians are time-served and fully qualified, all work is guaranteed and carried out to the 18th Edition Wiring Regulations, and risk assessments and method statements are issued before work is undertaken.

The range is deliberately broad — domestic, commercial and industrial installations, inspection and testing, EV charging, fire alarms and emergency lighting, security systems, data networking and maintenance — because buildings don't organise their problems by trade discipline, and you shouldn't need three contractors where one will do.

Much of our work comes through repeat business and referrals. In this trade, that's the only statistic that matters.

How we work

Advice, quality, aftercare

Professional advice first

Before, during and after installation. We'd rather talk you out of unnecessary work than invoice you for it — that's where the repeat business comes from.

Quality you can verify

NICEIC and SafeContractor accredited, 18th Edition compliant, RAMS before work, and certification you can hold in your hand. See our accreditations.

Aftercare that answers the phone

All work is guaranteed, and we're still here after handover — for the follow-up question, the tweak, or the next job.
