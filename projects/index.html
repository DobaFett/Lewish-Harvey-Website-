Commercial · Office · Newcastle

Office refurbishment fit-out

Client type

Professional services firm refurbishing occupied office space.

Challenge

A full electrical refit — lighting, power, data and emergency lighting — without stopping the business working around it.

Approach

Phased programme agreed up front with RAMS issued before work; circuits migrated area by area, out-of-scope faults flagged early with priced options.

Outcome

A modern, compliant installation — tested, certified to the 18th Edition and documented at handover.

Care & healthcare · North East

Care home compliance programme

Client type

Residential care provider with an occupied home and a busy inspection calendar.

Challenge

EICR, emergency lighting and fire alarm testing all falling due, plus an ageing nurse call system — with residents in place throughout.

Approach

Testing scheduled with the home's manager around routines and quiet hours; remedials prioritised by risk and priced clearly; nurse call maintained and faults resolved.

Outcome

Certificates in the logbook, remedials closed out, and a rolling testing schedule so nothing falls due unnoticed again.

  • Fire alarm testing

Domestic · Tyneside

Full house rewire

Client type

Family home with original wiring and a fuse board past its best.

Challenge

A complete rewire — new consumer unit, sockets and lighting throughout — in a house the family was still living in.

Approach

Room-by-room plan agreed in advance so power stayed on where it mattered; floors and decoration protected; the homeowner kept informed at every stage.

Outcome

A safe, modern installation certified to the 18th Edition — and a tidy house at the end of every working day.
