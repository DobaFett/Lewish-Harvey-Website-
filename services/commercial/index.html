Common questions

Commercial FAQs

Do you provide RAMS before starting work?

Yes. Risk assessments and method statements are issued before work is undertaken on every job — it's a standard part of how we work, not an extra.

What accreditations do you hold?

Lewis-Harvey is NICEIC and SafeContractor accredited. All work is carried out by time-served, fully qualified technicians to the 18th Edition Wiring Regulations, and all work is guaranteed. See our accreditations page for detail.

Can you handle installation and the ongoing testing afterwards?

Yes — that's the point of using one contractor. We install, then look after EICR inspections, PAT testing, emergency lighting and fire alarm testing, remedial works and planned maintenance schedules, so certificates never sneak up on you.

How does a site survey work?

We visit your site, walk the job with you, assess the existing installation and talk through what you're trying to achieve. You then get a clear written quote — with advice on options, not just a number.

Will the work disrupt our operations?

We plan the programme around your operations and agree scheduling with you in advance. Tell us your constraints at the survey — occupied buildings, term times, shift patterns — and we'll build the plan around them.

Do you work with architects and main contractors?

Yes. We price from drawings and specifications, attend design meetings where useful, and deliver certified electrical packages within larger programmes.

Got a commercial project or portfolio to look after?

Tell us what you're dealing with — we'll come back with clear advice and a written quote.
