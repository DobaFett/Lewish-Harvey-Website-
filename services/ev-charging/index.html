Common questions

EV charging FAQs

Can my home's electrical supply handle a charger?

That's exactly what the load check answers. Most homes can support a charge point, but the honest answer comes from checking your supply and existing demand first — which is why we survey before quoting hardware.

Why does the installer matter?

A charge point is a high-demand circuit running for hours at a time — it needs proper design, protection and certification. Ours are installed by fully qualified electricians, to the 18th Edition Wiring Regulations, by authorised home and workplace installers, and the work is guaranteed.

How many chargers can we fit at our workplace?

It depends on your site's supply and how it's already loaded. The survey tells us what's possible now and what a supply upgrade would unlock — we'll design a layout that can grow in phases rather than boxing you in.

Are there grants available?

Government schemes come, go and change their rules. Rather than promise something that may have closed, we'll check what currently applies to your circumstances and build it into your quote where eligible.

What happens if the charger develops a fault?

Call us — aftercare is part of the service. Because we installed and certified it, we know exactly how it was done and can diagnose quickly.

Start with a survey and a straight answer about what your site can support.
