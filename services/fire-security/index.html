Life-safety systems

Fire alarms & emergency lighting — installed and kept compliant

A fire system is only as good as its last test. We design and install fire alarm and emergency lighting systems, then keep them tested on a routine schedule — so escape routes stay protected and your logbook stays defensible.

  • Fire alarm installations & testing — design, installation, servicing and routine testing
  • Emergency lighting installations & testing — escape routes lit when it matters, tested on schedule
  • Remedials & upgrades — faults fixed and ageing systems brought up to standard
  • Testing schedules — combined with EICR and PAT programmes under one compliance arrangement

Why it matters

Compliance isn't optional — and neither is competence

Fire and life-safety systems carry legal responsibility for building owners and employers. Using one accredited contractor for installation and routine testing means clear records, one accountable team, and no gaps between "who fitted it" and "who checks it". Risk assessments and method statements come before work as standard, and everything is documented at handover.
